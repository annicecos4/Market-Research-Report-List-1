<p><p>The COVID-19 IgM/IgG rapid test kits market includes several types of testing methods. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) provide quick results through fingerstick blood samples, offering convenient point-of-care testing. Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA) are laboratory-based tests that detect antibodies in serum, providing higher sensitivity and specificity. Neutralization Assays assess the ability of antibodies to inhibit viral infection, offering insights into immune response effectiveness. Each method serves distinct purposes in diagnosing and monitoring COVID-19 infections.</p></p>
