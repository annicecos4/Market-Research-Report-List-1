<p><p>Vacuum cryo pumps are essential in creating deep vacuums for various industrial applications. The Injection Liquid Type uses a liquid refrigerant to cool surfaces and condense gases, achieving high vacuum levels efficient for applications like semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, the Closed Cycle Gas Type relies on a closed-loop system where a gas circulates, absorbing heat and maintaining lower temperatures to trap gases without the need for liquid refrigerants, making it suitable for sensitive environments and minimizing contaminants.</p></p>
