<p><p>Expression competent cells are engineered microorganisms used in molecular biology to facilitate gene expression and protein production. The market is primarily segmented into two types: chemically competent cells and electrocompetent cells. Chemically competent cells are treated with calcium chloride, enhancing their ability to take up plasmid DNA through heat shock. In contrast, electrocompetent cells utilize an electric field to induce DNA uptake, offering higher transformation efficiencies. Both types are essential for various applications in research and biotechnology, including cloning and protein expression.</p></p>
