<p><p>The Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) sensing market can be categorized into distributed sensing and fiber optic point sensing. Distributed sensing involves the use of FBGs along the length of a fiber to monitor changes in temperature, strain, and vibration over extensive areas, making it ideal for applications like structural health monitoring. In contrast, fiber optic point sensing focuses on specific locations for precise measurements, commonly used in fields such as aerospace, oil and gas, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
