<p><ul><li>Microfluidic dPCR</li><li>Droplet Digital PCR (ddPCR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital PCR (dPCR) market encompasses various types, notably microfluidic dPCR and droplet digital PCR (ddPCR). Microfluidic dPCR utilizes miniaturized fluidic components to partition samples, enabling precise quantification of nucleic acids, and is favored for high-throughput applications. In contrast, ddPCR organizes samples into thousands of droplets, allowing simultaneous amplification and quantification, thus enhancing sensitivity and accuracy. Both technologies support advancements in genomics, diagnostics, and research, catering to the growing demand for reliable quantitative analysis in molecular biology.</p></p>
