<p><p>The dehydrated green bell pepper market primarily comprises three types: powder, flakes, and granules. Dehydrated green bell pepper powder is finely ground and often used as a seasoning or ingredient in sauces and soups. Flakes are coarser and rehydrate easily, making them ideal for soups, stews, or as toppings. Granules fall between the two in texture and are versatile for both cooking and flavoring dishes. Each form caters to different culinary applications, enhancing convenience and shelf life.</p></p>
